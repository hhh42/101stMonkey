##Initial Impressions of MAAS It seems pretty complete. I’ve only focused on the mangement of virtual boxes current and that is obviously not its forte, though it seems rather suited to do that.

###Hardware I have some older hardware that I’ll try to PXE boot and wait on LAN. Until I wake cold hardware I won’t feel like I’ve really done what it’s made for.

###Juju Integration I like the seemingly perfect integration with Juju, rather encouraging. Though likely not where I’ll end up in the long run with my crazy hybrid environment. I may have to give up on trying to spin up stuff locally and setup a Juju in manual mode for all the local stuff I will effectively leave on while maybe a separate reagion in Digital Ocean when any scale is needed.
